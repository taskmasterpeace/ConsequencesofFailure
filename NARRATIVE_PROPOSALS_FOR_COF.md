# PROPOSAL 1: THE SPIRAL STRUCTURE

# PROPOSAL 4: THE DUAL TIMELINE STRUCTURE
## "Past and Future Collide"

This proposal uses Asha and the Chinese time traveler as FRAMING DEVICES.

### THE CONCEPT:

**EVERY EPISODE has THREE timelines:**

1. **THE PRESENT** - Main story happening now
2. **THE PAST** - Chinese time traveler exploring someone's history (flashbacks)
3. **THE FUTURE** - Asha scouting ahead, seeing consequences

**EXAMPLE EPISODE:**

- **Present:** Liu Xiao deciding whether to join moon rebellion
- **Past:** Chinese time traveler in Liu Xiao's past, watching him hide when CCP took sister
- **Future:** Asha in timeline where Liu Xiao stayed vs timeline where he left
- All three inform each other

**WHY THIS WORKS:**
- Gives you natural flashback structure
- Future glimpses create tension
- Time travelers become narrative glue
- Audience pieces together truth from three perspectives
