## THE GREAT SCRAMBLE - NEW CONCEPT

### WHAT IT IS:
After Rusty's warning and first LSW appear, EVERY government scrambles to find their Living Super Weapons.

### TIMELINE:
- **Year 1, Month 1-3:** Rusty warns world, most don't believe
- **Year 1, Month 4-6:** First LSW start appearing publicly
- **Year 1, Month 6+:** THE SCRAMBLE begins
- **Year 2-3:** Full-on global hunt for LSW

### WHAT IT LOOKS LIKE:
- Intelligence agencies tracking disappearances
- Military recruiting anyone who returns "changed"
- Black market emerges for LSW information
- Countries stealing each other's LSW
- Jackals (bounty hunters) emerge
- LSW going into hiding to avoid conscription

### DRAMA IT CREATES:
- "We found 3 LSW but they're a teacher, a plumber, and a teenager"
- "China has 50 LSW and we only have 12"
- "Our best LSW just defected to Russia"
- Treaties get signed trying to limit LSW per country (fails)

## MILITARY SERVICE ADVANTAGE - NEW WORLD-BUILDING

### THE CONCEPT:
Countries where military service is mandatory have an ADVANTAGE.

### ISRAEL EXAMPLE:
- Everyone serves in IDF
- When Grays choose Israelis, they're choosing people with military training
- Israeli LSW are already soldiers
- They know tactics, discipline, chain of command
- Israel punches above its weight because of this

### USA/OTHER COUNTRIES:
- No mandatory service
- LSW are random: musicians, teachers, accountants, mechanics
- They have godlike powers but NO combat training
- Creates massive training problem
- "You can lift a tank but you don't know how to clear a room"

### IMPLICATIONS:
- Some countries have soldier-LSW, others have civilian-LSW
- Creates different national challenges
- Israel, South Korea, Switzerland = trained LSW
- USA, UK, France = mixed bag
- Adds realism and drama

### WHO THE GRAYS CHOSE:
