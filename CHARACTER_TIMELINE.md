## YEAR 3 (VIRUS AND WARS BEGIN)

### Major Events
- **MAJOR VIRUS OUTBREAK**
- Regional wars erupt
- LSW as medical assets vs. military weapons debate
- First major civilian casualties from LSW collateral damage
- Sandra incident (kills Spanish First Lady)

### Character Appearances

**INTRODUCED:**
- **Verde (Brazil)** - Found wandering Amazon, fused with alien plant symbiont
- **Maria Carmen Quispe (Peru)** - Powers manifest during government massacre of protesters
- **President Carlos Mendoza (Spain)** - Wife Elena killed in Sandra's attack

**ACTIVE:**
- **Virus Response Heroes:** Johnny Rain (community organizing), Hank Foster (saves lives through "luck"), Japanese LSW Corps (spectacular response), Brazilian Civil Defense Corps
- **Virus Response Failures:** President Mugisha (Uganda), Iranian LSW, Spain (anti-LSW stance delays help)
- **Marcus Williams:** Focuses on protecting vulnerable communities from corrupt cops exploiting virus
- **Kaiser Eziobi:** Continues recruitment, intense philosophical debates with Marcus

**DEATHS:**
- **Elena Mendoza (Spain)** - President's wife, killed when Sandra's attack hits wrong hotel room
- Thousands from virus (millions globally)
- 47 dead at Carnaval, Rio (Tempestade incident)

### Key Moments
- **Sandra Incident:** Attacks room 408 targeting Spanish diplomat. Hotel double-booked—Elena Mendoza there instead. Elena and 5 others killed. President Mendoza transforms from moderate to obsessed.
- **Johnny Rain:** Community organizing during virus saves thousands in Uganda. Mugisha tries to take credit, Johnny exposes the lie.
- **Tempestade Incident (Brazil):** LSW goes rogue during Carnaval, kills 47. Military refuses civilian trial. Three generals threaten President Mendes with coup if he pushes. Mendes backs down.
- **Hank Foster:** His probability field creates "miracles" saving disaster victims. Faith crisis when children die despite his presence—temporary power loss from doubt.
- **Verde:** Begins attacking illegal logging, mining operations in Amazon. "Saint of the forest" or "demon of the trees" depending on perspective.
- **Maria Carmen Quispe:** Government forces massacre protesters. Her emotional amplification powers manifest, sends soldiers fleeing in terror. Tupac Katari Liberation Front forms.
- **President Mendoza:** Launches international manhunt for Sandra. Spain vs. Turkey crisis. NATO nearly fights itself.

### Regional Wars Begin
- Multiple theaters of conflict
- LSW deployed militarily
- Civilian casualties mounting

## YEAR 5 (KAISER'S DEATH - TURNING POINT)

### Major Events
- **KAISER EZIOBI KILLED BY STAMPEDE**
- Johnny Rain loses powerful protector
- Pan-African LSW network destabilized
- World descending into greater chaos

### Character Appearances

All major characters active, dealing with consequences of Kaiser's death and world deterioration.

### Key Moments
- **Kaiser's Death:** Lagos, Nigeria. Marcus "Stampede" Williams attempts to stop massacre/corrupt police operation. Kaiser Eziobi intervenes to prevent violence. Seven-minute fight. Kaiser killed. Marcus becomes America's most wanted supervillain. Johnny Rain devastated—loses protector.

- **Aftermath of Kaiser's Death:**
  - President Mugisha (Uganda) moves to arrest Johnny Rain
  - International pressure and mass protests prevent arrest
  - Johnny's music becomes more radical, global reach expands
  - Pan-African LSW network fragments without Kaiser's leadership
  - Marcus Williams disappears deeper underground

- **Team Aleph Breakdown (Israel):** 3 of 4 members dead (combat fatigue, PTSD, suicide). Sole survivor in psychiatric care. PM Yonatan Gold faces public criticism about Magen Program costs.

- **Iranian Counter-Success:** Arash's electromagnetic attack cripples Israeli power grid for 6 hours. First major Iranian LSW victory over Magen teams.

### Deaths
- **Kaiser Eziobi (Nigeria)** - Killed by Stampede in Lagos
- Team Aleph members (Israel)
- Continued war casualties

## YEAR 6 (WORLD FRAGMENTATION)

### Major Events
- Nation-states fragmenting
- LSW warlords emerging
- Alien invasion warnings intensify
- Civilian resistance movements grow
- Many characters at breaking points

### Character Appearances

**CRITICAL MOMENTS FOR:**
- Johnny Rain becomes de facto civilian resistance leader
- Japan's PM Akiko Tanaka faces no-confidence vote
- President Mugisha's regime fragility showing
- President Mendes (Brazil) watching country fragment
- PM Yonatan Gold considering nuclear/Threshold Treaty violations

### Key Moments
- **Uganda:** Regime fragments. Military considers coup. Johnny Rain leads civilian resistance with no powers, just organization.
- **Japan:** Coalition fractures. Conservative opposition growing. Article 9 amendment likely. Akiko Tanaka losing political battle but standing firm on principles.
- **Brazil:** Effectively fragmenting into regional power centers. Amazon states declare autonomy. President Mendes holds center through negotiation.
- **Iran:** Revolutionary Guard semi-autonomous. Supreme Leader Rahman controls less each day. LSW divided in loyalties.
- **Israel:** Surrounded by failed states, LSW warlords, Iranian expansion. Magen teams exhausted, some rebellious.

### Hunted
- Marcus "Stampede" Williams - SPEAR, governments, LSW bounty hunters worldwide
- Kali Mwangi - Kenyan government, SPEAR priority target
- Multiple underground LSW networks
