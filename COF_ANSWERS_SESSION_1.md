### Q39: Galloway vs Sapphire - THE FULL STORY!

**ANSWER:**

**It's Way Deeper Than Business:**

**Phase 1: The Taskmaster App**
- Vaughn Galloway and Charles Sapphire were best friends
- Created **Taskmaster app** together:
  - Hire people to do anything
  - Pay in Bitcoin - super anonymous
  - Blockchain verified - almost impossible to scam
  - Badge/trust system
  - **Could even hire for illegal stuff**
  - Had its own marketplace

**The First Betrayal:**
- Vaughn created **"Cheat Check"** service without telling Charles:
  - Hire actors to flirt with your significant other
  - Test if they'd be loyal
  - Could record it and talk to actor while they're flirting
  - Super lucrative service
- **Vaughn launched it without running it past Charles**
- Vaughn knew Charles would say no
- **Charles is religious** - doesn't believe in that negative stuff
- Vaughn "embraced the dark world of the Taskmaster app"

**The Split:**
- Charles: "We can't do this anymore, man"
- "You going behind my back... I can't do business with you like that"
- Both fell out
- Started saying "slick stuff" about each other online
- Two really rich dudes publicly beefing
- People loved it

**Phase 2: The Military Rivalry**
- Charles sees Vaughn gaining political influence with **his own private army**
- **FIST (First Infantry Strike Team)** - created BEFORE aliens
- Later renamed to **SPEAR** after aliens appeared
- Charles creates **Deck 52** as rival PMC

**Phase 3: The Todd Benchley Incident**
- Todd Benchley got captured by terrorists
- **Vaughn paid the $1.5M bounty** to free him
- Super controversial because:
  - It freed this hero who became Vaughn's bodyguard/operations head
  - **BUT it also funded the terrorist organization**
- Terrorist organization got enough money and reach "to really go crazy"

**THE TRAGEDY:**
- **That terrorist organization killed Charles Sapphire's wife (Emily)**
- This happened when Vaughn and Charles had already fallen out from business
- **This made the split permanent and personal**

**So the rivalry is:**
1. Business betrayal (Cheat Check)
2. Ideological differences (Vaughn = dark, Charles = religious)
3. Military competition (SPEAR vs Deck 52)
4. **Personal tragedy** (Vaughn's money indirectly funded Emily's killers)
