- Can expose operations planned before merge
- Knows current players and their methods

- Must supplement with current intelligence gathering
- Pattern recognition becomes more important than specific details

- Many operational details outdated
- Must rely heavily on pattern recognition
- Kaiser's snapshot advantage significantly reduced
- Must work harder to stay ahead

- Historical value only - most specifics outdated
- Personnel rotated, protocols changed, operations concluded
- Pattern recognition and strategic thinking now primary advantage
