Season 0: The Calm Before the Storm (Extended)


China - A renowned scientist, Dr. Li Zhang, discovers unusual signals emitting from space, but her findings are dismissed by her peers as mere satellite interference. Undeterred, Dr. Zhang secretly devotes herself to understanding these mysterious signals.


Brazil - In the heart of the Amazon rainforest, a tribe worships an ancient relic they call "The Gift from the Sky Gods." The relic is said to have fallen from the heavens thousands of years ago, hinting at the possibility of extraterrestrial contact with ancient civilizations.


Australia - A struggling journalist, Sarah O'Connor, stumbles upon a secret government project, Operation Outback, aimed at monitoring outer space for potential threats. Intrigued, she begins to investigate the truth behind the project and its connections to the alien threat.


Russia - A powerful crime lord, Boris Volkov, consolidates power in the Russian underworld, driven by an obsession with impending doom. While his criminal empire expands, he secretly funds research on advanced weaponry and technology.


Middle East - A displaced refugee, Amina Farid, miraculously survives a devastating bomb attack on her camp. Her newfound fame as a symbol of hope and resilience puts her in contact with an underground network of rebels, fighting against various political factions to unify their region against the alien threat.


Europe - International agencies join forces to create a specialized task force dedicated to combating human trafficking, terrorism, and cybercrimes. The task force, known as The Nighthawks, inadvertently uncovers evidence of a vast conspiracy, linking their investigations directly to the alien threat.


Japan - Through his company, Makino Tech, billionaire inventor and roboticist, Hideki Makino, revolutionizes the world of artificial intelligence, bio-mechatronics, and cybernetics. His innovations draw the attention of Vaughan Galloway, who sees potential in partnering with Makino to harness his technology for the fight against the alien threat.


As these events unfold across the world, international tensions rise, natural disasters intensify, and an aura of unease permeates throughout the global community. This sense of inevitable catastrophe will drive our heroes to come together, unite their skills and resources, and confront the impending alien threat head-on in Season 1.




Season 1: The Grays' Plan


US-After two years, Rusty Richards returns from the abduction by aliens and warns of a coming alien invasion. 

India- Major Raghavan Reddy returns  covered in a golden shape shifting substance that grants him god-like powers.


Interplanetary- The Grays begin to select some of the most capable and physically, mentally and emotionally resilient humans from all the countries and regions of the world and begin to genetically and technologically enhance them, turning them into living weapons.


US- Cuban Special Forces backed with the help of the country’s “Living Super Weapon”


The newly enhanced humans, known as Living SuperWeapons, are granted various superpowers the Grays hope to provide each of the 170 factions with their own weapons, depending on their population.


The United Nations passes the ‘Superhero Threshold Treaty’, regulating the use of superpowered individuals by member nations.


The United Nations rejects Vaughan Galloway's proposal for a special extraterrestrial organization


Vaughan Galloway forms SPEAR anyway as a privately owned organization in partnership with the United States of America. This is when Todd Shogun Bechnley, learns of the Vaugns true intentions the entire time.


Meanwhile, strange occurrences around the world involving alien lifeforms, technology, and phenomena continue to happen, causing fear and confusion among the general population.


Season 2: The Armada Arrives


Sages begin to appear, possessing supreme knowledge in one subject.


Uganda's Cissy Oliva engineers custom DNA animals, making the country one of the richest in Africa.


The Grays' plan is put into effect as they turn humanity into living superweapons and seek out other intergalactic species to provide assistance.


An Armada of four militarized alien races head towards Earth with the intention of making it unable to further pollute space.


The United Nations establishes a science branch, known as the Council of Scientific Advancement.


China discovers a way to manufacture superpowered beings and decides to use it to create a television show to promote patriotism and communism by having a contest where people compete for the position as the protector of China. The show is teased for almost half the year before being revealed towards the end of the season.




Season 3: The China Virus Outbreak


In the aftermath of the war with the Armada, a virus originating from China begins to spread rapidly across the globe.


The Sages, having successfully defended Earth from the Armada, now face a new threat in the form of the virus.


The virus causes chaos and devastation, leading to societal collapse and widespread panic.


The Sages, using their new abilities and weapons, work to contain the virus and to provide aid to those affected.


Rusty, now a trusted ally of the Sages, continues to assist them in their efforts to save humanity from the virus.


Overall, the story follows the journey of Rusty Richards and the Sages as they navigate a world threatened by alien invasions and deadly viruses, using their enhanced abilities and weapons to protect humanity. The Grays' plan to turn humanity into living superweapons provides a unique twist to the traditional alien invasion narrative, while Rusty's personal struggles and doubts add depth and complexity to the story.
