## <a name="establishment-24"></a>1. ESTABLISHMENT 24 (INDIA)

### ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW
**Type:** Government-aligned LSW organization
**Country:** India
**Alignment:** Extension of India's military and intelligence services

### LEADERSHIP
**Colonel Raghavan Reddy**
- Strong, charismatic leader
- Die-hard patriot
- No-nonsense attitude
- Has earned both friends and enemies in high places

**Major Gupta**
- Colonel Reddy's right-hand man
- Second in command

### ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE
- **Hierarchy:** Traditional military structure
- **Leadership:** Col. Reddy at top, Maj. Gupta as second
- **Team Size:** 12 core agents who carry out orders
- **Members:** Mix of military personnel, government officials, and private citizens
- **Total Size:** Believed to be in the hundreds

### MISSION & VISION
**Mission:** To make India a safe and prosperous country for all Indians by any means necessary

**Vision:** To improve the quality of life for all Indians and establish India as a leading world power

**Core Values:**
- Patriotism
- Dedication
- Sacrifice

### DOCTRINE & IDEOLOGY
- Believes LSW (Living Superweapon) citizens are vital to India's future
- Should be utilized to their fullest potential to improve the country
- Focus on using abilities for infrastructure and social good

### MAJOR ACHIEVEMENTS

**Infrastructure Projects:**
- Improved electricity systems
- Enhanced railways
- Upgraded roads, ports, airports
- Improved irrigation systems
- Enhanced water supply and sanitation
- Framework recognized internationally for using LSW citizens to improve quality of life

**Icchatam Ganga Project** ("The Wishing Ganga")
- Most ambitious project to date
- Extracted impurities from the Ganga river
- Successfully removed close to 80% of waste and pollutants
- Widely lauded internationally

### METHODS & APPROACH
- **NOT willing to:** Violate international law or harm innocent civilians
- **Willing to:** Work with other countries and organizations to further goals
- Uses data and analytics to improve military decision-making
- Analyzes past conflicts to understand enemy operations
- Establishes best practices for Indian military

### SECRETS & CLASSIFIED OPERATIONS

**Assassination Operations:**
- Credited with assassinations of:
  - Paresh Baruah
  - Dawood Ibrahim
- Has an assassination squad under Col. Reddy and Maj. Gupta

**Kargil War Intelligence:**
- Provided critical intelligence during Kargil War
- Helped Indian military drive out Pakistani forces from Kargil district
- Accomplished WITHOUT needing to deploy LSW soldiers

**Technology Innovation:**
- One of first organizations to use drones for various missions
- Pioneered data analytics for military decision-making
- Uses technology for social good

### SPECIALTY DIVISIONS

1. **Assassination Squad**
   - Reports directly to Col. Reddy and Maj. Gupta
   - Handles high-profile targets

2. **Data and Analytics Division**
   - Analyzes past conflicts
   - Establishes best practices for Indian military

3. **Drone Division**
   - Uses drones for various missions
   - Both military and civilian applications

### OPERATIONS & PROCEDURES

**Mission Assignment:**
- Missions come EXCLUSIVELY from Government of India
- Private companies and individuals CANNOT request services

**Recruitment Conditions:**
- Not publicly known
- Believed to require special skill or talent
- Training is intense and secretive
- Involves physical, mental, and emotional tests

**Member Benefits:**
- Access to exclusive information and resources
- Mentorship from experienced members
- Opportunity to make a difference

**Risks:**
- Physical danger in high-risk missions
- Emotional stress
- Risk of being killed in line of duty
- Lack of public recognition (operates in secrecy)
- Danger of discovery

### RELATIONSHIP WITH GOVERNMENT
- Close relationship with Indian government
- Established by government
- Receives majority of funding from government
- Given great leeway in operations as long as goals are accomplished

### RELATIONSHIP WITH LSW CITIZENS
- Good relationship
- Understands importance of utilizing LSW abilities
- Works to integrate them into society productively

### PUBLIC PERCEPTION
- Largely unknown entity despite successes
- Good reputation in international community
- Success rate not publicly known
- Work often done in secrecy

### CHALLENGES
- Risk of mission failure on high-risk operations
- Lack of public recognition for members
- Operating in secrecy while maintaining effectiveness
- Balancing international law with national interests

### ULTIMATE GOAL
Protect the interests of India and its citizens through:
- High-risk missions
- Data collection and analysis
- Strategic military support
- Infrastructure development
