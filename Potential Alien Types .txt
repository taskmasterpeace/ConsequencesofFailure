Type 1:
* Humanoid, but also have a primary form of pure energy 
* Telepaths without mouths
* Arrive in an Armada of spaceships 
   * Solar ray deflectors
   * Infinite power supply
   * Black out the sky over cities
   * Suck power from the cities below
* Energy feeders that feed on electrical currents


Type 2:
* Upper half is humanoid, snake-like from the waist down
* Can see interdimensionally 
* Can smell heat and fear
* Armada of ships 
   * Feed on heat
   * Attempt to increase global warming (and therefore heat) by pumping huge amounts of smog into the atmosphere
   * Attempting to make earth dry and hot like their home planet
* Predatory meat eaters


Type 3:
* Translucent humanoids – can see straight through their skin
* Bioweapons that put plants and wildlife into states of rapid evolution, causing them to become faster, smarter, and more aggressive to humans
* Eventual goal is to guide the earth itself to overrun humanity and wipe it out 
* Ship Armada -
   * Sprays the chemical bioweapon everywhere it goes 
   * Does not affect humans, only plants and animals
