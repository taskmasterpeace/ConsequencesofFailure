Jerome Davis's Background
Occupation: Jerome is a skilled electrician with decades of experience, known for his problem-solving skills and innovative approaches to complex electrical systems. He takes pride in his work, embodying the essence of human ingenuity and craftsmanship.

Introduction to AI: Jerome's company introduces an AI system designed to optimize electrical work, promising efficiency and safety improvements. Jerome is asked to assist in training the AI, sharing his knowledge and experience to improve the system's performance. He sees this as an opportunity to contribute to his field's advancement, unaware of the implications.

Betrayal: A year and a half after the AI's implementation, Jerome finds himself laid off as the company decides it can reduce costs by relying on AI-guided robots for the majority of electrical work. This betrayal hits hard, undermining Jerome's life's work and casting him adrift in a world that seems to no longer value human skill and craftsmanship.
